How It Works

Welcome! Please go through the following requirements to better understand how I am able to help you lead better lives. As much as I would like to reach out to each and every one of you, it is not feasible. Hence, the criterion.

Eligibility

  1. English language Fluency
  2. Must be in age group of 25-60 years
  3. PHQ (Personal Health Questionnaire) has to be filled out with accuracy and to the best of your knowledge
  4. PHQ will thereafter be assessed and once I ascertain that I am the right person to help you (at my sole discretion), I will approve it and you will then be guided further through the process.
  5. Once approved, an email notification will be sent with a unique username and password
  6. Password can be changed, as per your convenience at a later point of time
  7. Once you login with your unique username and password, you will be guided to a drop-down menu of consent, payment portal and thereafter booking of sessions
  8. Your ten (10) sessions will consist of half an hour video calls with me
